The Shadow Code

Our guild is built on loyalty and trust. As such, every member is expected to act with the guiding principles of loyalty, integrity, and excellence. We have also set forth The Shadow Code. Any member acting against The Shadow Code will be expelled and forfeits all membership benefits.

Do no harm to the guild

  • Do not attack or steal from guild members.
  • Do not interfere with an active job.
  • Respect alliances.

Protect the innocent

  • Do not attack or steal from the poor or misfortuned.
  • Work to correct injustices.
  • Subvert oppression.

Support fellow guildmates

  • Always aid a guild member in need.
  • Share your knowledge.
  • Be willing to learn.
