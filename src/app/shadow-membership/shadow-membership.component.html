The Shadow Loyal

Membership is open to all beings with a drive to pursue knowledge and defend freedom.

Members of The Shadow Loyal have full autonomy and can choose to contribute to our efforts through various means. Some may choose to operate as Star Loyal employees, promoting education or engineering ship customizations. Others may focus on technical covert operations or stealth combat tactics.

Fun

SLY members have the coolest role in the universe. Their role within the guild is dynamic, and they are encouraged to change focus as they see fit. Stagnation leads to complacency, and it is our job to continuously push one another to greater levels of success.

Free Training

Members will have direct access to experts in various fields who will act as mentors. Our guild will host a training compound to perfect our craft, and even novices will be relied upon during guild jobs.

Funded Missions

All guild-sanctioned jobs are fully funded. The guild will make every effort to reimburse any expenses incurred.

Discounted Services

Services such as repair, refuel, rescue, modifications, and upgrades are available to all guild members at the cost of materials.

Structure

SLY is built on a flattend structure where all members have equal say. Rather than a ranking system, all members are encouraged to select a specialization. Members with the same specialization will train together to hone their craft. They will be called upon to share their knowledge and to apply their skills when necessary.

Shadow Missions

Some jobs need to get done even if there is no specific client making the request. Shadow Missions act as a live training exercise and provide supplementary guild revenue. The guild receives a portion of the profits and the remainder is divided evenly between participants.
